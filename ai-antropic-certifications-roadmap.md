Here is the comprehensive **Suggested Learning Roadmap** for all Anthropic Academy certifications, organized by career role and technical depth. All courses are free, self-paced, and include official certificates upon passing the final quiz.

## 1. The "Zero-to-Hero" Developer Roadmap (15–20 Hours)
*Best for: Software Engineers, AI Builders, and CTOs wanting to build production agents.*

This path takes you from basic usage to architecting complex, multi-agent systems with external tool connections.

**Phase 1: Core Foundations**
Start with **Claude 101** to understand the interface, followed immediately by **Building with the Claude API**. The API course is the critical 8+ hour investment covering authentication, streaming, and tool use.
*   *Prerequisite:* Basic Python/JSON knowledge.

**Phase 2: Agentic Workflows**
Move to **Claude Code in Action** to master the terminal-based developer loop. Then, specialize in automation with **Introduction to Agent Skills** (creating reusable `SKILL.md` files) and **Introduction to Subagents** (delegating tasks between agents).
*   *Prerequisite:* Git and terminal familiarity.

**Phase 3: Advanced Integration (MCP)**
Complete the **Introduction to Model Context Protocol (MCP)** to connect Claude to external data sources. Finish with **MCP Advanced Topics** for production-grade deployment, sampling, and transport implementations.
*   *Prerequisite:* Python, HTTP basics, and completion of the API course.

## 2. The Product & Strategy Roadmap (4–6 Hours)
*Best for: Product Managers, Team Leads, and Non-Technical Founders.*

This path focuses on strategic implementation, workflow integration, and understanding capabilities without writing code.

**Phase 1: Fluency & Framework**
Begin with **AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations** to master the "4D" competencies (Delegation, Description, Discernment, Diligence). Follow with **Claude 101** for hands-on interface practice.

**Phase 2: Workflow Automation**
Take **Introduction to Claude Cowork** to learn desktop automation, file management, and plugin setup for daily productivity.

**Phase 3: Infrastructure Strategy (Optional)**
If your team uses specific cloud providers, take **Claude with Amazon Bedrock** or **Claude with Google Cloud Vertex AI** to understand enterprise deployment, security, and cost implications.

## 3. The Role-Specific Fluency Roadmap (1–3 Hours)
*Best for: Students, Educators, Nonprofit Leaders, and Small Business Owners.*

Select the single track that matches your profession to maximize immediate impact.

*   **Students:** Take **AI Fluency for Students** (30 mins) for research integrity and study skills.
*   **Educators:** Complete **AI Fluency for Educators** (1 hour) followed by **Teaching AI Fluency** to design AI-integrated assessments.
*   **Nonprofits:** Enroll in **AI Fluency for Nonprofits** to optimize fundraising and operations.
*   **Small Business:** Take **AI Fluency for Small Businesses** for practical workflow adoption.

*Note: It is recommended to take **AI Capabilities and Limitations** (15 mins) as a prerequisite for any role-specific track to understand risk and bias.*

## Enrollment & Certification Steps

1.  **Register:** Go to `anthropic.skilljar.com` and sign up with any email address (no credit card required).
2.  **Enroll:** Select any course from the catalog; all are listed at $0.
3.  **Learn:** Complete the video modules and reading materials at your own pace.
